Uniform & Professional Appearance

**Standard:** Ushers must wear a clean, consistent uniform (e.g., matching shirts, vests, badges) to be easily identified by guests.

**Attitude:** Maintain a **polite, calm, and professional demeanor** even under pressure.

Mandatory Pre-Event Training

**Requirement:** A minimum 1-hour briefing covering venue map, roles, safety protocols, and how to handle difficult guest interactions.

**Communication:** Assign **Team Leaders** to whom ushers can report issues quickly via radio/phone.

Emergency & Safety Liaison

**Duty:** Ushers are the first link to safety. They must know the location of fire exits, first-aid stations, and the designated emergency assembly point.

**Protocol:** Report any suspicious activity, medical emergencies, or safety hazards immediately to Security/First Aid.

Post-Event Duties (Teardown Support)

**Role:** Assisting guests with departures, ensuring lost-and-found items are collected, and often helping with initial light teardown tasks (e.g., collecting handouts, folding chairs).

**Final Check:** Sweeping the area for misplaced personal belongings or important materials.
