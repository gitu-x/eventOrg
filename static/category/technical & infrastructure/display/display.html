Projectors (Brightness & Resolution)

**Key Factor:** **Lumens (Brightness)** must match the room's ambient light. Choose high **Resolution (1080p or 4K)** for crisp graphics and video.

**Use:** Large screens where high brightness is critical (e.g., conferences, movie nights).

LED Walls / Video Walls

**Key Factor:** Offers superior **brightness, contrast, and seamless scaling** without shadows. Excellent for brightly lit rooms and outdoor events.

**Use:** Concerts, large corporate backdrops, outdoor screenings, live broadcasting events.

Projection Screens (Type & Size)

**Key Factor:** Choose **Rear Projection** (projector behind screen) for a clean stage look and to prevent speaker shadows. Size should be 1/6th of viewing distance.

**Use:** Conference presentations, background scenery, simultaneous content display.

Video Switcher / Scaler

**Key Factor:** Allows for **seamless transition** between multiple input sources (laptops, cameras, videos) without the screen going black (glitching).

**Use:** Any event with more than one presenter or multiple video sources.

**Key Factor:** Small monitors placed **on the floor, facing the speaker**. They display the current slide and/or speaker notes privately.
