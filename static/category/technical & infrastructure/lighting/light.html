Wash Lighting (Ambient)

**Purpose:** To provide **general, wide-area illumination** with a soft, even color. It sets the overall mood and base color of the room.

**Use:** Covering the entire stage or walls in a single color. Essential for corporate meetings and receptions.

Spotlighting (Key Focus)

**Purpose:** To sharply **illuminate a specific person or object**, drawing the audience's attention instantly. Typically a white or warm light.

**Use:** Highlighting speakers at the lectern, cake cutting at a wedding, or an award recipient.

**Purpose:** Lights placed on the floor, aimed upwards to **decorate walls, pillars, or drapes**. Adds depth and height to the venue.

**Use:** Transforming a plain banquet hall into a vibrant space; aligning light color with event theme.
