Vehicle Types & Fleet Selection

**Variety:** Choose based on guest volume and event type: **Coaches/Buses** (large groups), **Mini-vans** (mid-size transfers), **Luxury Sedans** (VIP/Individual transfers).

**Requirement:** Vehicles must be well-maintained, air-conditioned, and have valid commercial permits.

Schedule Planning & Timing

**Crucial:** Create a detailed **Pick-up/Drop-off Schedule** with buffer time for traffic. For shuttles, define fixed, frequent intervals between locations (e.g., hotel to venue).

**Communication:** Clearly share the schedule with guests, hotels, and all drivers.

Airport & Station Arrivals

**Protocol:** Dedicated staff must be positioned at the airport/station with **name placards** or identifiable uniforms/badges.

**Info:** Staff need real-time flight/train tracking to manage delays and coordinate driver movements.

Late-Night & Final Departures

**Safety:** Ensure the late-night departure area is **well-lit and secure**. For events with alcohol, ensure drivers are sober and briefed on safety.

**Logistics:** Have more vehicles ready for the final peak rush after the event concludes.

Venue Parking & Staging Area

**Planning:** Designate a clear, accessible area near the venue for **Guest Drop-off/Pick-up** (no parking allowed) and a separate, secured area for the **Drivers/Fleet to wait (Staging)**.

**Valet:** If offering Valet Parking, ensure adequate, trained staff and secure key management.

Route Planning & Traffic Management

**Preparation:** Drivers must be given maps and alternative routes to avoid known traffic choke points or construction zones.

**Liaison:** Coordinate with local traffic police or venue security for smooth road access during peak arrival/departure times.

VIP/VVIP Protocol & Escort

**Service:** Provide dedicated, high-end vehicles (e.g., SUV, luxury sedan) and professional, discreet drivers for VIP guests.

**Safety:** Use a **security escort** or a lead car if required, and ensure the driver knows the confidential itinerary.

Transportation Manager & Staffing

**Team:** A single **Transportation Manager** must oversee all logistics, drivers, and guest queries. Assign staff to high-traffic areas (lobby, departure gate).

**Communication:** All staff and drivers must have working **radios/mobile phones** and a master contact list.
